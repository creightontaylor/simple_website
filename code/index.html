Welcome to Sif Task Force - Integrating AI and Expertise in Project Management

Phases of Operation

Discussion

This phase involves initial discussions about project requirements, goals, and strategies. It's a collaborative effort between Customer Agents and Worker Agents to ensure a clear understanding of the project's objectives.

Assignment

During the assignment phase, tasks are distributed among team members based on their expertise and the project's needs. This ensures that each task is handled by the most qualified individual or AI agent.

Execution

The execution phase is where the actual work begins. Tasks are carried out according to the plan, with continuous monitoring and adjustments as necessary to ensure successful completion.
